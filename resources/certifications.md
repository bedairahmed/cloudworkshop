## Microsoft Azure Certifications

Azure certifications are organized into three levels, each building on the previous. They are role-based, meaning each cert aligns with a specific job function.

### Certification Levels

| Level | Badge | Description |
|-------|-------|-------------|
| **Fundamentals** | ![Fundamentals](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/media/learn/certification/badges/microsoft-certified-fundamentals-badge.svg) | Entry-level. Validates foundational knowledge. No prerequisites. Never expires. |
| **Associate** | ![Associate](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/media/learn/certification/badges/microsoft-certified-associate-badge.svg) | Mid-level. Validates hands-on skills for a specific role. Must renew annually (free). |
| **Expert** | ![Expert](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/media/learn/certification/badges/microsoft-certified-expert-badge.svg) | Advanced. Validates architecture & design expertise. Requires an Associate cert as prerequisite. Must renew annually (free). |

## Exam Tips

1. **Use official free resources first** ‚Äî Microsoft Learn, Google Cloud Skills Boost, and Linux Foundation free courses are the best starting points
2. **Hands-on practice is essential** ‚Äî Azure offers a free trial with $200 credit; GCP offers $300 credit for new accounts
3. **Take practice exams** ‚Äî Microsoft and Google provide free practice assessments for every exam
4. **Register with a personal email** ‚Äî For Microsoft exams, use a personal Microsoft account (not work/school) to keep your credentials if you change jobs
5. **Digital badges** ‚Äî All providers issue digital badges you can share on LinkedIn to showcase your skills
6. **Renewal** ‚Äî Microsoft Fundamentals certs never expire; Associate/Expert renew annually via a free online assessment. CNCF certs are valid for 3 years. DCA is valid for 2 years.
7. **Study groups** ‚Äî Consider forming a study group with your workshop peers to stay motivated
